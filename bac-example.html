Balihoo Help

Campaigns are the heart of the Balihoo platform. These marketing campaigns are made up of one or more tactics, executed individually according to their individual schedules and targeting, all focused on the objectives of the campaign. A campaign could include multiple emails, display ads, and paid search ads all working together.
To create a new campaign, click the Create New Campaign button.
Once you execute a campaign, it goes into Active status and runs continuously (according to its tactics schedules) until you stop it. You can make changes to a campaign at any time (including stopping a campaign) by clicking the Edit button for the campaign. You can also archive inactive campaigns to remove them from this list.
To view results for a campaign, click the Report icon. The report will show how the campaigns tactics are performing so that you can edit the campaign to maximize its return on investment.
For more information about campaign strategies, or if you have further questions, consult the Balihoo FAQ or contact support@balihoo.com.
